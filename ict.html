About
ICT Forum

ICT forum will share issues of key players in the AI industry (infrastructure, solution, application) and seek possibilities for mutual cooperation between Korean and US tech companies. Discussion will also highlight emerging new businesses, technologies, products, and services in the post-pandemic “un-tact” world, as well as AI industry’s new opportunities
